There are three levels to choose from: \"lite\", \"normal\", and \"heavy\".

- "normal" is suitable for scenarios that may require calling tools, such as providing deterministic responses to knowledge concepts, using tools to perform operations, or general web searches;
- "heavy" is suitable for research-oriented tasks and complex compound operational processes, usually involving long-term processing tasks.
